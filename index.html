Publications

Methionine restriction for cancer therapy: From preclinical studies to clinical trials

This publication explores the potential of methionine restriction (MR) as a promising approach for cancer therapy. It highlights how MR impacts tumor metabolism, delays cancer progression, and enhances the effectiveness of conventional treatments. The study bridges insights from preclinical models to ongoing clinical trials, emphasizing the therapeutic benefits and future possibilities of incorporating MR in personalized cancer treatment.



ADAMTS-13 Behavior in Thrombocytopenia of Infectious Origin in ICU Patients Indian Journal of Critical Care Medicine, 2025

This study evaluates factors influencing door-to-balloon (D2B) time in patients with ST-elevation myocardial infarction (STEMI). By analyzing retrospective data, it identifies key determinants that affect timely intervention, emphasizing the importance of minimizing D2B time to improve patient outcomes. The findings contribute valuable insights for optimizing emergency cardiac care and enhancing clinical protocols..



Antibiotic-Dispensing Patterns and Awareness of Anti-microbial Resistance Among the Community Pharmacists in South-Central India

This study analyzes the antibiotic dispensing practices of community pharmacists in South-Central India and evaluates their awareness of antimicrobial resistance (AMR). It highlights prevalent dispensing patterns, identifies knowledge gaps, and emphasizes the need for stricter antibiotic stewardship and better pharmacist education to combat the growing threat of AMR effectively...
